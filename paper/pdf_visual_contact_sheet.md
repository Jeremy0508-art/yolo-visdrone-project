# PDF Visual Contact Sheet

This file is generated by `tools/render_pdf_review_contact_sheet.py`.

The contact sheet is a visual aid for manual review of the current manuscript PDF. It does not replace page-by-page inspection before final submission.

## Inputs

- PDF: `paper\manuscript_submission_candidate.pdf`
- Pages: 15
- Render zoom: 0.35
- Columns: 5

## Outputs

- Contact sheet: `paper\figures\pdf_review\manuscript_pages_contact_sheet.jpg`
- Contact sheet size: 271.6 KB

## Review Use

1. Open the contact sheet to quickly scan for blank pages, oversized figures, table overflow, and figures floating into the conclusion or references.
2. If any page looks suspicious in the contact sheet, open the original PDF and inspect that page at full size.
3. Record the final manual page-by-page result in `paper/CEA_PDF_VISUAL_REVIEW_FORM.md`.
